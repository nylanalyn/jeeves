# Themed Injuries Reference

A quick reference showing how injuries change across different quest themes.

## Noir November Theme

| Injury Name | Description | Duration | Effects |
|------------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Pistol Whipped | Someone got the drop on you - your head is ringing like church bells. | 2 hours | -50% XP |
| Knife Wound | A blade found its mark in the alley. You're bleeding but still standing. | 4 hours | No energy regen |
| Bourbon Hangover | That bottle seemed like a good idea last night. It wasn't. | 1 hour | No energy regen, -25% XP |
| Cigarette Burn | They used you as an ashtray during the interrogation. | 3 hours | -40% XP |
| Broken Ribs | Someone worked you over real good. Every breath is agony. | 6 hours | No energy regen |

## December Shopping Theme

| Injury Name | Description | Duration | Effects |
|------------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Paper Cut | Gift wrapping has drawn blood - the paper cuts sting with every movement. | 2 hours | -50% XP |
| Sore Feet | Hours of mall walking have left your feet screaming for mercy. | 4 hours | No energy regen |
| Stress Headache | The combination of crowds, noise, and pressure has resulted in a pounding headache. | 1 hour | No energy regen, -25% XP |
| Cookie Burn | A baking mishap has left you with a painful burn. Should have used oven mitts. | 3 hours | -40% XP |
| Wallet Strain | Your wallet feels so light it hurts. The financial stress is weighing on you. | 6 hours | No energy regen |

## Sci-Fi/Cyberpunk Theme

| Injury Name | Description | Duration | Effects |
|------------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Neural Feedback | Corrupted data has fried your synapses - everything feels fuzzy. | 2 hours | -50% XP |
| System Overload | Your augments are running hot and need time to cool down. | 4 hours | No energy regen |
| Code Injection | Malicious code is wreaking havoc on your systems. | 1 hour | No energy regen, -25% XP |
| Data Corruption | Your memory banks are scrambled - nothing makes sense right now. | 3 hours | -40% XP |
| Power Drain | Something is leeching energy from your core systems. | 6 hours | No energy regen |

## Notes

- All themes maintain the same injury **mechanics** (durations and effects)
- Only the **flavor** changes (names and descriptions)
- This ensures balance while providing thematic variety
- Players can have up to 2 of the same injury type active at once
- 50% base chance to receive an injury on defeat
- Armor can reduce injury chance
- Medkits heal one injury at a time
